Dice Game

Welcome to the dice game

.The rule is simple,players roll the dice, the points are accumulated and stored in the current score.
.The first player to score 100 points wins.
.To transfer the points to the global score, hold down the HOLD, then pass the hand to another player.
.Be careful, if the die falls on 1, you will see your points disappear in the current score and pass your turn.
